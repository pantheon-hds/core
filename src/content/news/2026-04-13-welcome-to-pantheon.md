Pantheon is now live in closed beta.

If you are reading this, you are one of the first people to step inside. The platform is real, the ranks are real, and the community you help build here will shape everything that follows.

## What you can do right now

- **Connect your Steam account** and receive your rank automatically based on achievements
- **Apply to become a Judge** and help verify challenge submissions from other players
- **Explore the leaderboard** and see who has already earned their place in the Pantheon

## What is coming next

The challenge system is the heart of Pantheon. Community-created challenges, blind judging, and verified ranks — the full loop from submission to statue. The first challenges are being prepared and will go live soon.

The six Paths — Warrior, Judge, Teacher, Lorekeeper, Chronicler, Orator — will open one by one. Warrior and Judge are available now. The others are in development and will be shaped together with the community.

## A note from the founder

This project has no investors, no advertising, and no pay-to-win mechanics. Everything here is built on three words: Honor, Democracy, Skill.

Welcome to the beginning.

*— Voland*
