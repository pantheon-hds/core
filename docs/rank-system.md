# Rank System

## Core philosophy

Every rank is earned. Not purchased. Not given.
Gold is the beginning. Legend is decided by the people.

---

## Rank tiers

### Bronze
- Entry level rank
- Player is learning the game
- Has some official achievements
- Assigned manually or by partial Steam achievement completion

### Silver
- Player has completed a significant portion of official achievements
- Shows dedication and game knowledge

### Gold
- **Automatically assigned via Steam API**
- Player has unlocked 100% of official Steam achievements
- No manual review required
- This is where the community journey begins

### Platinum
- First community-driven rank
- Player completes challenges created by the community
- Requires video proof + blind anonymous voting (3 of 5 judges)
- Examples: no-leveling run, all bosses without flasks, speedrun under time limit

### Diamond
- Advanced community challenges
- Higher difficulty threshold
- Requires video proof + blind anonymous voting (4 of 5 judges)
- Reserved for challenges that very few players can complete

### Legend
- **Decided entirely by the community — no exceptions**
- Player submits a unique super-challenge of their own design
- Community of Gold+ players votes openly
- No time limit on voting — discussion encouraged
- Identity is visible at this stage — the achievement speaks for itself
- Once granted, the Legend rank and statue are permanent and unique

---

## Statues

Each rank comes with a unique statue displayed on the player's profile.

| Rank | Statue style |
|------|-------------|
| Bronze | Rough cast warrior — simple, unfinished |
| Silver | Detailed soldier — clean lines, no decoration |
| Gold | Armed knight — full armor, golden finish |
| Platinum | Glowing artifact — light emanating from within |
| Diamond | Crystalline figure — geometric, sharp, cold light |
| Legend | Unique — one of a kind, never repeated, chosen by community |

Legend statues are never duplicated. Each one is a permanent piece of platform history.

---

## Festival ranks

During platform festivals, special limited statues are available.
- Only obtainable during the festival window
- Cannot be earned after the festival ends
- Verified through the same blind anonymous voting system
- Prizes may be awarded by community or platform partners (devices, games, gear)

Festival statues carry the festival name and season permanently.

---

## Rank display

- Every player profile shows their highest rank per game
- All statues earned are displayed in the player's Hall of Statues
- Legend players are listed in the Pantheon — visible to all
- Ranks are game-specific — a Legend in Elden Ring is separate from a Legend in Hollow Knight

---

## Community challenge creation

- Any Gold+ player can propose a new challenge
- Challenges are voted on by the community before becoming official
- Challenge difficulty is tagged by the community (Platinum / Diamond / Legend-worthy)
- Low quality or duplicate challenges are removed by community moderation

---

## Notes

- Ranks cannot be purchased under any circumstances
- Supporter Pack purchases have zero effect on rank progression
- A player's rank history is permanent and public
- Rank stripping is possible only in cases of proven cheating — decided by community vote
