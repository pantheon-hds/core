# Rank System

## Core philosophy

Every rank is earned. Not purchased. Not given.
Gold is the beginning. Legend is decided by the people.

---

## Rank tiers & degrees

Each rank except Grandmaster and Legend has three degrees (I, II, III).
Total progression steps: 22

```
Bronze       I → II → III
Silver       I → II → III
Gold         I → II → III
Platinum     I → II → III
Diamond      I → II → III
Master       I → II → III
Grandmaster  (single tier)
Legend       (single tier — community vote only)
```

---

## How to progress within a rank

### Bronze I → Bronze II → Bronze III
- Bronze I: created account, connected Steam
- Bronze II: unlocked 25% of Steam achievements in a game
- Bronze III: unlocked 50% of Steam achievements in a game

### Silver I → Silver II → Silver III
- Silver I: unlocked 75% of Steam achievements
- Silver II: unlocked 90% of Steam achievements
- Silver III: unlocked 95% of Steam achievements

### Gold I → Gold II → Gold III
- Gold I: 100% Steam achievements — auto-assigned via Steam API
- Gold II: completed 1 community Platinum challenge
- Gold III: completed 3 community Platinum challenges

### Platinum I → Platinum II → Platinum III
- Platinum I: completed 1 Diamond challenge
- Platinum II: completed 3 Diamond challenges
- Platinum III: completed 5 Diamond challenges

### Diamond I → Diamond II → Diamond III
- Diamond I: completed 1 Master-tier challenge
- Diamond II: completed 3 Master-tier challenges
- Diamond III: completed 5 Master-tier challenges

### Master I → Master II → Master III
- Master I: completed 1 Grandmaster-tier challenge
- Master II: completed 3 Grandmaster-tier challenges
- Master III: completed 5 Grandmaster-tier challenges + community recognition

### Grandmaster
- Completed all Master III requirements
- Recognized by community vote
- One of the top players in the game globally

### Legend
- Decided entirely by the community — no exceptions
- Player submits a unique super-challenge of their own design
- Community of Gold+ players votes openly
- No time limit on voting — discussion encouraged
- Identity visible — the achievement speaks for itself
- Once granted, permanent and unique forever

## Evidence & storage policy

### Bronze → Gold
- No video required
- Auto-verified via Steam API
- No storage needed

### Platinum → Diamond
- Video required during voting period
- Stored for 30 days after rank granted
- After 30 days player may keep or delete video — their choice
- Community voted and confirmed — that is sufficient proof

### Master → Grandmaster
- Video required
- Stored for 90 days
- Highlight reel (5-10 min) required alongside full video
- Timestamped key moments required

### Legend
- Full video must remain public permanently
- YouTube public (not unlisted)
- Automatically archived to archive.org
- Video metadata stored in database forever
- This is platform history — it must be preserved

## Complaint & appeal system

### Filing a complaint
- Available within 30 days of rank being granted
- Must specify exact timestamp and describe the violation
- Vague complaints are automatically rejected
- Example of valid complaint: "At 2:14:33 the HUD shows a mod indicator"

### Complaint review
- New set of 5 random judges review the specific moment
- Blind anonymous process — same as original voting
- If violation confirmed → rank review opens to full community vote
- Even rank removal is democratic — not automatic

### After 30 days
- Rank becomes permanent if no complaints filed
- Legend rank — complaints accepted indefinitely given permanent status

## AI verification roadmap

| Rank | Verification method | Timeline |
|------|-------------------|----------|
| Bronze–Gold | Steam API automatic | Now |
| Platinum–Diamond | Vision AI (HUD analysis) | Phase 2 |
| Master–Grandmaster | Vision AI + human confirmation | Phase 2 |
| Legend | Human community only — forever | Always |

### Phase 2 — Vision API
- GPT-4 Vision or Gemini Video
- Checks HUD elements: flask counter, level, timer, death count
- Returns verdict with explanation and timestamp evidence

### Phase 3 — Custom trained models
- Train game-specific models on verified submissions
- Each game gets its own model that knows its UI
- Accuracy improves with every new verified submission
- Target: 95%+ automatic verification for Platinum and Diamond

### Why Legend stays human forever
Legend is not about verifying facts.
It is about recognizing greatness.
No AI can decide that. Only people who love the game can.

## Notes

- Ranks cannot be purchased under any circumstances
- Supporter Pack has zero effect on rank progression
- Rank history is permanent and public
- Rank stripping requires community vote — never automatic
- All ranks are game-specific — Legend in Elden Ring ≠ Legend in Hollow Knight
